Rules

In the Pig game, players take turns rolling a standard die. Each roll's outcome adds to the player's turn score, but rolling a 1 ends the turn and cancels the accumulated points for that turn. Players can choose to hold and add their turn score to their total score, passing the turn to the next player. The objective is to reach a set target score (e.g., 100 points) to win. Players can continue rolling, risking their current turn score, to accumulate points strategically and be the first to reach the target score.
